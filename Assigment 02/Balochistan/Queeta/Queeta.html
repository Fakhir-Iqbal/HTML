Queeta

Quetta is known as the fruit basket of Pakistan producing plums, peaches, pomegranates, apricots, apples, melons, cherries, pistachios and almonds. Saffron grows very well on mountains around 5000 high and is cultivated on a commercial scale. The yellow and red varieties of tulip grow wild around Quetta.There are many beautiful places to visit in Quetta. Although the city was severely affected by Quetta Earthquake in 1935, it has been restored. Quetta is a city worth seeing for the people who have an attraction for history and tourism.Quetta is one of the coldest places in Balochistan and Pakistan.
